About Me

From circuits to classrooms — building real-world tech education

I'm Devashish Markam — a builder, educator, and founder based in Chhattisgarh. I started Novaforge Labs to fill a gap I kept seeing: engineering students who could pass exams but couldn't wire a circuit or train a model.

My workshops are hands-on from day one — no slides-only sessions. Students walk in, build something, and leave understanding why it works.

  • Diploma in Electrical Engineering (CSVTU) + B.Sc Biology
  • AWS Certified Machine Learning – Specialty
  • 5× Meta Certified (Digital Marketing & Advertising)
  • Founder of Vouchr — hyperlocal talent network, Chhattisgarh
  • Tech Content Creator (Reels & YouTube Shorts)
